Search

Playlist

Other
  • To search for videos, type in keywords what you are looking for. Press "Enter" or
    to confirm your input.
  • If you want to load more videos hit the "Load more" button on the bottom of the search results.
  • When you have found some good videos, and you want to find similar videos; simply press the "Search related" button (!Coming soon!).
  • To create a playlist, click underneath the video screen. Slothyx will automatically add a new playlist to your playlist library.
  • Deleting a playlist is as simple as creating one. Press the "Thrash" symbol right besides the "Add" button.
  • If you want to fill your playlist with content, add videos by clicking the "Add to current playlist" button beneath the video.
  • To delete videos, press the red next to the title of the video.
  • Sharing a playlist is as easy as clicking 2 buttons. Hit the "Generate code" button below the "load" and "search" input box. Copy playlist code (Mark all - "STRG + A"; Right click "copy"; or strg+c.).Insert the code on facebook, skype, email, or similar.
  • If you want to use a code, copy it! When copied, insert him in the "Load" input box, below the search field and confirm it by pressing "Enter" or
    .
  • To display videos on another screen, press the "Remote player" button underneath the video.
