Get started with slothyx.com

Playlist

Other
  • To search for videos, type in keywords what you are looking for. Press "Enter" or
    to confirm your input.
  • If you want to load more videos hit the "Load more" button on the bottom of the results-field.
  • To create a playlist, click underneath the video screen. Slothyx will automatically add a new playlist to your playlist library.
  • Deleting a playlist is as simple as creating one. Press the "Wheel" symbol to the right of the "Playorder" drop-down menu.
  • If you want to fill your playlist with content, add videos by clicking the "Add to current playlist" besides beneath the video.
  • To delete videos, press the red next to the title of the video.
  • Sharing a playlist is as easy as clicking 2 buttons. Hit the "Generate code" button below the "load" and "search" input box. Copy playlist code. You can generate your code as well, by using the settings menu next to the "Playorder" drop-down menu. Insert the code on facebook, skype, email, or similar.
  • If you want to use a code, copy it! When copied, insert it in the "Load" input box, below the search field and confirm it by pressing "Enter" or
    .
  • To display videos on another screen, press the "Remote player" button in the settings menu.
