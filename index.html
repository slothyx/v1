  • To search for videos, type in keywords what you are looking for. Press "Enter" or to confirm your input.
  • If you want to load more videos hit the "Load more" button on the bottom of the results-field.
  • When you have found some good videos and you want to find similar videos simply press the "Search related" button.
  • To create a playlist, click underneath the video screen. Slothyx will automatically add a new playlist to your playlist library.
  • Deleting a playlist is as simple as creating one. Press the "Wheel" symbol to the right of the "Playorder" drop-down menu.
  • If you want to fill your playlist with content, add videos by clicking the big + besides the search result.
  • To delete videos from your playlist, press next to the title of the video.
  • Sharing a playlist is as easy as clicking one button. Hit the "Share playlist" button in the options wheel. Copy the code. Share it!
  • If you want to use a code insert it in the "Load" input box besides the search field and confirm it by pressing "Enter" or
    .
  • To display videos on another screen, press the "Remote player" button in the settings menu.
